## 🤝 WHY 12 MONTHS? (THE REAL REASON)

We're not asking for a 12-month commitment to trap you. Here's what it actually does for **YOU**:

### 1. **We Learn Your Brand Inside-Out**
Months 1-3 are about understanding your voice, audience, and what works. By Month 4, we're not guessing — we're *executing*. A shorter contract means we'd still be learning when it ends.

### 2. **We Invest in Your Success**
With a 12-month partnership, we can:
- Dedicate a team to your account (not shuffle freelancers)
- Buy equipment optimized for your content style
- Build custom templates that match your brand
- Track what works and double down on it

### 3. **Better Results = Better ROI**
Social media algorithms reward consistency. Posting 90 videos/month for 12 months builds momentum, audience trust, and platform favor. Stopping at Month 6 resets that progress.

### 4. **Locked-In Rate (No Surprises)**
Your RM 22,000/month is fixed for 12 months. No inflation adjustments, no "rate reviews," no surprise price hikes — even if our costs go up.

### 5. **Priority Support**
12-month partners get:
- Dedicated Project Manager (direct WhatsApp access)
- Faster turnaround on rush requests
- First priority during peak seasons (Raya, CNY, 11.11, etc.)
