## 🚦 CRITICAL DECISION POINTS

| Month | Decision | Trigger | Options |
|-------|----------|---------|---------|
| **M3** | Continue or pivot? | If client happy + margins >40% | Scale / Renegotiate / Exit |
| **M6** | Add second client? | If team at <80% capacity | Yes / Wait until M9 |
| **M9** | Raise prices? | If demand > capacity | +20% for new clients |
| **M12** | Replicate or exit? | If model works | Clone / Sell / Franchise |

### Decision Framework

**M3 — Continue/Pivot:**
- ✅ Continue if: Client NPS > 8, margins > 40%, team retention 100%
- ⚠️ Renegotiate if: Margins < 30%, revision creep, payment delays
- ❌ Exit if: Client toxic, chronic late payments, scope creep uncontrolled

**M6 — Second Client:**
- ✅ Add if: First client stable, team at 60-70% capacity, you at <15 hrs/week
- ⚠️ Wait if: First client still volatile, key staff at risk of burnout
- ❌ Don't add if: You're still doing daily ops, cash reserve < 2 months

**M9 — Raise Prices:**
- ✅ Raise if: 80%+ capacity, waitlist forming, strong case studies
- ⚠️ Hold if: Market soft, client budget constraints known
- ❌ Don't raise if: Client relationship fragile, competitive pressure high

**M12 — Replicate/Exit:**
- ✅ Replicate if: Model proven, systems documented, team can self-manage
- ✅ Sell if: Multiple acquisition offers, you want to move to next venture
- ✅ Franchise if: Model location-agnostic, strong brand, training materials ready
