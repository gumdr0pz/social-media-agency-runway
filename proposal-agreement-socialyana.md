## 3. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 3.1 Service Description

The Service Provider shall provide the following services:

| Service Component | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **Video Production** | Creation of short-form video content for social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook, YouTube Shorts) |
| **Scripting** | Development of video scripts, hooks, and captions |
| **Editing** | Video editing including transitions, B-roll, colour correction, and sound design |
| **Captions & Subtitles** | On-screen text captions in Bahasa Malaysia and/or English |
| **Platform Optimization** | Content formatted and optimized for each platform's specifications |

### 3.2 Deliverables

The Service Provider shall deliver **90 (ninety) videos per month** as detailed in **Schedule 1**.

### 3.3 Video Specifications

| Specification | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Video Length** | 15-60 seconds per video (unless otherwise specified) |
| **Format** | Vertical 9:16 (TikTok/Reels) and/or horizontal 16:9 (YouTube) |
| **Resolution** | Minimum 1080p (Full HD) |
| **Delivery Format** | MP4, H.264 codec |
| **Turnaround Time** | 5-7 working days from brief approval to first draft |

### 3.4 Client Responsibilities

The Client shall:

(a) Provide clear creative briefs for each video batch at least 10 working days before required delivery date

(b) Provide all necessary brand assets (logos, brand guidelines, colour palettes, fonts)

(c) Designate a single point of contact for approvals and communications

(d) Provide raw footage if required (if Client-supplied footage, production time reduced by 40%)

(e) Review and provide feedback on drafts within 3 working days of submission

## 4. TERM AND COMMITMENT

### 4.1 Initial Term

This Agreement shall commence on **[●] 2026** ("Commencement Date") and shall continue for a minimum period of **12 (twelve) months** ("Initial Term").

### 4.2 Annual Commitment Discount

In consideration of the Client's commitment to the 12-month Initial Term, the Service Provider shall apply a discount to the Standard Monthly Rate as follows:

| Package | Standard Monthly Rate | Annual Commitment Rate | Discount | Annual Savings |
|---------|----------------------|----------------------|----------|----------------|
| **Standard Package** (15 videos/month) | RM 3,500 | RM 3,080 | 12% | RM 5,040 |
| **Volume Package** (90 videos/month) | RM 25,000 | RM 22,000 | 12% | RM 36,000 |

### 4.3 Pricing Rationale

The discount structure is calculated as follows:

- **Standard per-video rate:** RM 233 per video (RM 3,500 ÷ 15 videos)
- **90-video monthly rate at standard pricing:** RM 20,970 (90 × RM 233)
- **Agreed Volume Package rate:** RM 25,000/month (includes premium services, priority turnaround, dedicated account management)
- **Annual Commitment Rate (12% discount):** RM 22,000/month
- **Total Annual Value:** RM 264,000
- **Total Annual Savings to Client:** RM 36,000

### 4.4 Renewal

Upon expiry of the Initial Term, this Agreement may be renewed by mutual written consent of both Parties. The Parties agree to negotiate renewal terms at least 60 days prior to expiry.

### 4.5 No Automatic Renewal

This Agreement shall **NOT** automatically renew. Any renewal must be documented in a written addendum signed by both Parties.

## 6. REVISIONS AND APPROVALS

### 6.1 Revision Allowance

The Client is entitled to **2 (two) rounds of revisions** per video at no additional cost.

### 6.2 Revision Process

(a) **Round 1:** Client provides consolidated feedback within 3 working days of first draft delivery.

(b) **Round 2:** Service Provider incorporates feedback and resubmits. Client provides final feedback within 2 working days.

(c) **Additional Revisions:** Any revision rounds beyond 2 shall be charged as per Clause 5.3.

### 6.3 Approval Deemed

If the Client does not provide feedback within the stipulated timeframes, the Deliverable shall be deemed approved and accepted.

### 6.4 Scope Creep Protection

Feedback that introduces new creative directions, additional elements, or changes to the originally approved brief shall be considered a new request and may incur additional charges as per Clause 5.3.

## 8. DATA PROTECTION AND PDPA COMPLIANCE

### 8.1 Compliance with PDPA

Both Parties acknowledge and agree to comply with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Act 709) and all amendments thereto, including the 2024 amendments introducing mandatory breach notification requirements.

### 8.2 Data Collection and Processing

(a) **Lawful Basis:** The Service Provider shall only collect, process, and store personal data as necessary for the performance of this Agreement and with the Client's consent.

(b) **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data shall only be used for the specific purposes outlined in this Agreement.

(c) **Data Minimisation:** Only personal data that is necessary and relevant shall be collected.

### 8.3 Data Security

The Service Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage, including:

(a) Encryption of data in transit and at rest

(b) Access controls and authentication measures

(c) Regular security assessments

(d) Staff training on data protection

### 8.4 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, the Service Provider shall:

(a) Notify the Client within **72 hours** of becoming aware of the breach

(b) Provide details of the breach, including categories of data affected and approximate number of data subjects

(c) Cooperate with the Client in notifying the Personal Data Protection Department and affected data subjects as required by PDPA

### 8.5 Data Subject Rights

The Service Provider shall assist the Client in fulfilling data subject rights requests, including access, correction, and deletion requests, within the timeframe required by PDPA.

### 8.6 Data Retention

Personal data shall be retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes of this Agreement, and in any event for no longer than **7 (seven) years** from the termination of this Agreement (to comply with Malaysian tax and commercial law requirements).

### 8.7 Cross-Border Data Transfer

The Service Provider shall not transfer personal data outside Malaysia without the Client's prior written consent and ensuring adequate protection measures are in place as required by PDPA.

## 10. TERMINATION

### 10.1 Termination for Convenience

**During Initial Term:** Given the 12-month minimum commitment, neither Party may terminate for convenience during the Initial Term except as provided in Clause 10.3.

**After Initial Term:** After the Initial Term, either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving **60 (sixty) days** prior written notice.

### 10.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately by written notice if the other Party:

(a) Commits a material breach of this Agreement and fails to remedy such breach within **14 (fourteen) days** of receiving written notice specifying the breach

(b) Becomes insolvent, enters into liquidation (voluntary or compulsory), or has a receiver appointed over its assets

(c) Ceases to carry on business

### 10.3 Early Termination Penalty (Client-Initiated)

If the Client terminates this Agreement during the Initial Term without cause (i.e., not due to Service Provider's material breach):

(a) **Months 1-6:** Client shall pay **50% of the remaining contract value** for the Initial Term

(b) **Months 7-12:** Client shall pay **25% of the remaining contract value** for the Initial Term

This early termination payment is a genuine pre-estimate of the Service Provider's loss, including:
- Lost revenue from the committed term
- Costs of dedicated resources allocated to the Client
- Opportunity cost of declining other clients
- Staffing and equipment investments made in reliance on the contract

### 10.4 Termination for Service Provider Breach

If the Service Provider commits a material breach that cannot be remedied or is not remedied within 14 days:

(a) Client shall be entitled to a pro-rata refund of prepaid fees for undelivered services

(b) Service Provider shall deliver all completed but unpaid Deliverables

(c) No early termination penalty shall apply

### 10.5 Effects of Termination

Upon termination or expiry:

(a) The Client shall pay all outstanding fees due up to the termination date

(b) The Service Provider shall deliver all completed and approved Deliverables

(c) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party's Confidential Information

(d) Clauses 7 (Intellectual Property), 8 (Data Protection), 9 (Confidentiality), 11 (Liability), and 12 (Governing Law) shall survive termination
