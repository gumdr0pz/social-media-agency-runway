## 📋 ROLE DESCRIPTIONS

### 1. VIDEO EDITOR (Full-Time)

**Hire:** Month 1 + Month 7  
**Salary:** RM 4,000 - 5,000/month  
**Reports to:** You (M1-6), Project Manager (M7+)

#### Responsibilities
- Edit 45-50 videos/month (22-25 videos/editor when 2 hired)
- Add captions, transitions, music, branding
- Export in multiple formats (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
- Maintain brand consistency across all content
- Archive and organize raw footage

#### Requirements
- 2+ years video editing experience (social media focus)
- Proficient in Adobe Premiere Pro / Final Cut / DaVinci Resolve
- Fast turnaround (24-48 hours per video batch)
- Portfolio showing Reels/TikTok/Shorts work
- Can work independently with minimal direction

#### Nice to Have
- Motion graphics skills (After Effects)
- Thumbnail design experience
- Understanding of social media algorithms
- Experience with batch editing workflows

#### Interview Test
- Provide 10 min raw footage → Edit into 30-sec Reel with captions
- Time limit: 2 hours
- Evaluate: Speed, creativity, technical skill, attention to detail

### 2. CONTENT CREATOR (Full-Time)

**Hire:** Month 2  
**Salary:** RM 3,500 - 4,500/month  
**Reports to:** You (M1-6), Project Manager (M7+)

#### Responsibilities
- Write scripts for 90 videos/month (or adapt client-provided briefs)
- Film talking head + B-roll content (10-15 videos per session)
- Coordinate with talent/subject matter experts
- Light editing (rough cuts for editor to polish)
- Manage content calendar and shooting schedule

#### Requirements
- 1-2 years content creation experience
- Comfortable on camera OR directing talent
- Basic videography skills (lighting, framing, audio)
- Scriptwriting ability (conversational, engaging)
- Organized, can manage multiple deadlines

#### Nice to Have
- Experience in client's industry
- Social media strategy knowledge
- Photography skills
- Owns basic equipment (can start immediately)

#### Interview Test
- Write 3 script variations for a 30-sec product promo
- Film a 1-min intro video using provided equipment
- Evaluate: Writing quality, on-camera presence, technical execution

### 3. PROJECT MANAGER (Part-Time)

**Hire:** Month 4  
**Salary:** RM 2,000 - 2,500/month (0.5 FTE, ~20 hrs/week)  
**Reports to:** You  
**Location:** Remote OK

#### Responsibilities

#### Nice to Have
- Social media agency background
- Video production knowledge
- Client-facing experience
- Flexible hours (can respond to client outside 9-5)

#### Interview Test
- Given 90-video/month calendar with 3 deadlines missed → How do you recover?
- Draft client email explaining 2-day delay with revision requests
- Evaluate: Communication tone, problem-solving, professionalism

## 🎯 HIRING MILESTONES

### Month 1: Video Editor #1

**Goal:** Prove you can hit 90 videos/month with 1 editor + you  
**Success Metrics:**
- [ ] 90 videos delivered by Month 1 end
- [ ] <5% rejection rate from client
- [ ] Editor can work independently after Week 2

**Red Flags:**
- Editor needs constant hand-holding
- Turnaround time >48 hours per batch
- Quality inconsistent across videos

**Contingency:** If Editor #1 fails, use freelancers while re-hiring (budget RM 150/video × 90 = RM 13,500/month stopgap)

---

### Month 2: Content Creator

**Goal:** Remove you from scripting/filming workflow  
**Success Metrics:**
- [ ] Creator handles 80% of scripting by Week 3
- [ ] Creator handles 100% of filming by Week 4
- [ ] You spend <5 hrs/week on production

**Red Flags:**
- Scripts need heavy rewriting
- Uncomfortable on camera or directing talent
- Misses shooting deadlines

**Contingency:** You continue scripting; hire freelance videographer per session (RM 300-500/session)

---

### Month 4: Project Manager (PT)

**Goal:** Remove you from daily client ops  
**Success Metrics:**
- [ ] PM handles 100% of client communication
- [ ] You review <10% of videos before delivery
- [ ] Zero missed deadlines in first month

**Red Flags:**
- Client complains about communication gaps
- PM escalates every small issue to you
- Deadlines slip without early warning

**Contingency:** You remain primary contact; delay PM hire until Month 6

---

### Month 7: Video Editor #2

**Goal:** Prepare for scale (120+ videos/month)  
**Trigger:** Only hire if:
- [ ] Editor #1 at 80%+ capacity consistently
- [ ] Second client signed OR upsell confirmed
- [ ] Cash reserve > RM 100k

**Success Metrics:**
- [ ] Both editors hit 45-50 videos/month each
- [ ] No quality drop with increased volume
- [ ] Team can handle 120 videos without overtime

## 🚩 INTERVIEW CHECKLIST

### For All Roles

- [ ] Portfolio review (must show relevant work)
- [ ] Practical test (paid, 2-4 hours max)
- [ ] Reference check (2 previous employers)
- [ ] Trial period (2 weeks, paid, clear KPIs)
- [ ] Contract signed (probation 3 months)

### Cultural Fit Questions

1. *"Describe a time you missed a deadline. How did you handle it?"*
2. *"What's your preferred way to receive feedback?"*
3. *"Tell me about a project where you had to juggle multiple priorities."*
4. *"Why do you want to work at a small agency vs. a big company?"*
